## What this service is

The service provides technical access to proxy configurations for personal
and lawful use. It is not presented as a tool for violating access restrictions,
evading Russian regulatory requirements, or carrying out unlawful activity.

We do not guarantee uninterrupted availability, fixed speeds, or compatibility
with every ISP, device, or client app.

## What data we process

We try to avoid collecting unnecessary data. We do not build advertising
profiles and we do not use customer data for self-promotion or unsolicited
marketing.

The service may process only the minimum technical data required to issue
access, keep user records, and prevent abuse:

- your `Telegram ID`;
- the fact that you started the bot and a basic user record for accounts that
  started the bot;
- technical identifiers sent by client apps, such as `User-Agent` values from
  `Koala-Clash`, `FlClashX`, and similar clients;
- service data about access status, expiry, and technical errors.

Some of this data may be processed by Telegram infrastructure and by a backend
based on the project. We do not use this data for
targeted advertising and we do not sell it to third parties for marketing.

## What we do not intentionally collect

Unless required by law, service security, or technical necessity, we do not
collect or sell:

- browsing history;
- traffic contents, messages, or files;
- standalone marketing profiles;
- data for disclosure to third parties for advertising purposes.

- Roskomnadzor by-laws related to access restrictions, technical control, and
