Use `alerts` channel to stay updated on service status and important project updates. This channel is subscriber-only. To get access, open [Get access](/faq/customer/get) and use `Channel` button in bot's chat.

![Alerts channel invite button](/images/alerts/invite.png)

<Info>
  This is a broadcast channel. You receive official updates quickly, without support chat noise.
</Info>

![Alerts channel screenshot](/images/alerts/channel.png)

## What we post

- Incident alerts when service quality drops or access is unstable.
- Planned maintenance windows with expected impact and timing.
- Security and policy notices that affect customer usage.
- News and announcements about releases, changes, or milestones.
- Resolution updates when incidents are closed.

## How to use the channel

1. Join `alerts` channel in Telegram.
2. Set notifications to **All messages** if you need immediate updates.
3. Pin the channel so you can check status quickly during incidents.
4. Use support for help requests. Keep `alerts` for announcements only.

## Update format

Most posts include:

- Affected service or feature.
- Expected customer impact.
- Next update time when available.
