## What to expect

- Concise and practical documentation.
- Focus on **clarity over completeness**.
- Topics based on real needs, not theoretical coverage.
- Occasional opinions grounded in experience.

## Language support

This documentation is available in English and Russian. You can switch language from the top navigation.

The documentation does not aim to spoon-feed. The goal is to guide, encourage exploration, and help build real understanding.

This wiki will continue evolving with the tools, workflows, and problems we face.
