## Battery drain

If you recently purchased a Nothing Phone, it might be running a very old firmware version, which can cause weird performance. Increased battery consumption may occur until you update to the latest version.

*We also recommend doing the following:*

<Steps>
<Step title="Reset Nothing Launcher state">
Clear cache and storage for Nothing Launcher.
</Step>

<Step title="Reset Google Play Services state">
Clear cache and delete data for Google Play Services (if OS is freshly installed).
</Step>

<Step title="Clear AR services cache">
Clear cache for Google Play Services for AR.
</Step>

<Step title="Restrict launcher battery mode">
Set battery usage mode for Nothing Launcher to `RESTRICTED`.
</Step>

<Step title="Remove launcher notification access">
Disable notification access for Nothing Launcher.
</Step>

<Step title="Restart and fully charge">
Restart and fully charge your phone.
</Step>
</Steps>

## Chargers

The phones support Quick Charge 4.0 and Power Delivery 3.0 charging protocols.

- Nothing Phone 1: 33 Watts
- Nothing Phone 2, 2a: 45 Watts

All phones support Qi standard wireless charging up to 15 Watts.
Recommended GaN charger brands:

- Baseus
- Anker
- Ugreen

The author has been using the Baseus GaN5 Pro 2C+U charger for a year.

There are no exact recommendations. Choose a charger based on your needs, such as number of ports, size, and power.

## Disabling call recording notification during a call

To disable the voice notification that call recording has started, follow these steps:

<Steps>
<Step title="Install TTSLexx">
Install **`TTSLexx`** from **Google Play**.
</Step>

<Step title="Open Text-to-Speech settings">
In phone settings, search for **`Text-to-Speech`** and open it.
</Step>

<Step title="Switch engine to TTSLexx">
In **Text-to-Speech** settings, set **`Default Text-to-Speech Engine`** to **`TTSLexx`**.
</Step>

<Step title="Reset Phone app cache and storage">
Go to **Apps**, open the **Phone app**, then tap **Clear Cache** and **Clear Storage**. Contacts remain safe (confirmed for Google Phone app).
</Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
    The call recording notification may appear during the first call, but it usually will not occur in subsequent calls.
</Note>
